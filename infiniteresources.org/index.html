Global Trade in a new era of multilateralism

We are an independent trading house specialising in the procurement, logistics and movement of waste and recycling products.

We focus on facilitating waste paper, scrap rubber and alternative fuels' movement.

We ensure compliance to the highest degree while placing a great emphasis on creating value for all stakeholders along the value chain.

We are an independent trading house specialising in the procurement, logistics and movement of waste and recycling products.

What We Do

We combine our extensive market knowledge with our established global network to reliably procure, inspect, trade and transport physical commodities from places they are plentiful to where they are most needed.

Finance

Finance

We bridge the finance gap ever present in this industry. We create value by financing the movement of the commodities from the supplier to the customer, therefore facilitating the trade.

Shipping

Shipping

Our long-standing relationships with global shipping lines allow us to provide competitive pricing, higher service levels, and increased flexibility.

Manoeuvrability

Manoeuvrability

Our global network enables us to provide maximum value to our suppliers and customers by connecting the most suitable markets attaining maximised market value. We also help navigate dynamic pricing and market volatility.

Service

Service

We add value to the global trade of waste recyclables with exceptional service and performance across the supply chain. Our team maintains legislative compliance to the highest degree to ensure economically sound and environmentally safe practices.
