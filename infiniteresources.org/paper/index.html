PAPER

We specialise in the global buying and selling of Waste Paper.

We are directly involved in procuring and supplying waste paper to responsible recyclers, who use the waste paper to create an economically sound and environmentally safe output.

Our major sourcing markets include UK, Europe and the Americas, whilst our sales markets focus on supply across Asia.

Our products include OCC, Soft Mixed Paper, Hard Mixed Paper, Magazines, Sack Kraft, Reels, Polycup etc.
